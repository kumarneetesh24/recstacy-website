Recstacy Gymkhana

Recstacy, the annual Social and Cultural festival of NIT Durgapur, is all set to mesmerize you with its ever growing magic in the fields of music, dance, theatre, photography, literature, fine arts, quizzing and debating... Technology Students' Gymkhana is the hub of the numerous extra-curricular and co-curricular activities in NIT Durgapur ranging from sports to socio-cultural. From its inception in 1952, the Gymkhana has played a key role in the everyday lives of Dgpians cultivating and nurturing their extra-curricular talents.

The moto of Technology Students' Gymkhana is "Yogah Karmasu Kausalam" which in English means "Perfection in action is Yoga". The Technology Students' Gymkhana, IIT Kharagpur is managed by the students, for the students, under the guidance and active participation of the faculty and staff members.
